-ˏˋ POMOPY: An old-school solution for a modern problem ˊˎ-

A fun ASCII-art-filled, customizable time-management solution to optimize productivity. The Pomodoro Technique was developed by Francesco Cirillo in the 1980s and emphasizes working and taking breaks in regular intervals. 

POMOPY FEATURES:
This Pomodoro timer saves your preferences in a file locally so that you can start and stop easily. Use the `help` command to see all of the available commands.
